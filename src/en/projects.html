Proyectos diferentes

SHOES FOR LOVE
This is a Project that helps reduce levels of inequality, providing new shoes or tennis shoes to children in the peripheral neighborhoods of Medellín, dignifying their quality of life and their good development. If you also want to be part of this project and change the lives of many children, contact us!
FOOD FOR LOVE
This project seeks to benefit vulnerable families, bringing food to different homes in Medellín, whose economic conditions do not allow them to acquire basic necessities. If you want to be part of the change, contact us!
